Our story

The real heart
of Cham

Cafe Luzia has been the neighbourhood's favourite gathering spot since 2008. What started as a simple ice cream parlour grew into a full café — a place where everyone is welcomed with a smile, regulars feel like family, and the coffee is always perfect.

Our owner, lovingly called "Grandma" by loyal guests, pours her heart into every cup and every sandwich. The warm bread rolls you didn't order but always receive? That's just how we do things here.

Our origins

From ice cream parlour
to neighbourhood soul

Cafe Luzia opened its doors in 2008 as a small gelateria on the central square of Cham. From the very first day, the owner — Luzia herself — believed that a great cafe is about more than food. It is about the feeling of being welcome.

Over the years the menu expanded, the terrace grew, and loyal regulars became lifelong friends. But the soul stayed exactly the same: honest ingredients, handmade with care, served with a genuine smile.

Today Luzia is known throughout the region for her legendary gelato, her artisan espresso, and the free bread rolls that appear on your table before you even ask.
